A Very Cheesy Quesadilla


Go back to the list

Description

Have you ever wondered, "How can I show off my lactose tolerance to all my firends"? No? Well here is a very cheesy quesadilla recipe that you can enjoy should you ever want to find out just how much cheese can put into one quesadilla. This is a hearty meal but not for those who don't absolutely love cheese. It's ricey, meaty, and most of all absolutely cheesy.

Servings: 1 quesadilla

Ingredients

  • A lot of cheese, shredded or sliced per your preference
  • 1 cup of cooked rice warmed
  • 2 raw tortillas
  • 2 tacos worth of your favorite taco meat

Steps

  1. Place a suitably sized pan on the stove at medium heat
  2. Put one tortilla down and cover with a substantial amount of cheese
  3. Evenly spread rice out over the cheese
  4. Gently press your meat of choice into the rice, making sure to keep all ingredients within the boundaries of the tortilla
  5. Add a generous helping of cheese to the top and cover with the final tortilla
  6. Flip when the bottom tortilla starts to brown and the bottom layer of cheese is melted about 3 to 5 minutes
  7. Once both tortillas are browned to your liking, plate and eat hot. Cutting is optional but it does make it easier to eat.
  8. Enjoy!
